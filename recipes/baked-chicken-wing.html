Baked Chicken Wing Recipe

Back to home

Description

These easy chicken wings are coated with a blend of spices, garlic, and olive oil and baked in the oven until crisp, flavorsome, and delicious. No one will be able to resist!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, pressed
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 10 chicken wings

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Combine olive oil, garlic, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a large, resealable bag; seal and shake to combine.
  3. Add chicken wings to the bag; reseal and shake to coat evenly.
  4. Arrange chicken wings on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until crisp and cooked through, about 30 to 45 minutes.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
